**Description of reference image:**

The background consists of bare tree branches against a clear blue sky, indicating an outdoor winter setting. A golden bow and greenery (likely part of a Christmas decoration) are visible in the lower-left corner, suggesting a festive context.

Thinking process:  
The editing instruction explicitly states the following transformations:  
- Change Santa’s attire to an **18th-century explorer’s coat with leather details** → the current red suit must be replaced.  
- Replace the **red hat** with a **tricorn hat** → the current Santa hat must be removed and substituted.  
- Replace the **white gloves** with **leather gauntlets** → the gloves are to be changed.  
- Change the **background** to a **rugged, snowy mountain landscape** → the current natural trees-and-sky background is to be replaced entirely.  

These changes indicate that the **entire costume and environment** are subject to transformation. However, no mention is made of altering the **subject’s facial features**, **beard**, **glasses**, or **posture**. The instruction does not specify changing the **identity** of the person (i.e., still representing a figure in historical disguise), nor does it suggest modifying the **gesture** or **facial expression**.

Therefore, only elements directly specified for replacement are allowed to change. All other visual components not mentioned in the instructions must be preserved unless logically affected by the changes.

Allowed Changes List:  
- The red velvet suit (including fur trim)  
- The red Santa hat with white fur and pom-pom

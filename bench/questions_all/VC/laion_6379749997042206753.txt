  - The **presence of both raw and cooked popcorn** ensures the story of transformation is complete.  

- **Non-modified element deduction:**  
  - While the **scattered kernels** and **wooden surface** may be stylized, their presence supports realism and continuity. They should remain in the scene to ground the whimsy in the original setting.  
  - The **color palette** (oranges, browns, greens) is critical to the autumn theme and brand consistency — it must be retained, even if enhanced.

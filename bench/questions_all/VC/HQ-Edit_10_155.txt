While the background tiles may become streets, the **design motif** (geometric Islamic patterns) should still reflect the original aesthetic coherence. Thus, while not identical, the **visual language of the tiling** should persist in the new setting.

After removing allowed changes (e.g., background, table, addition of figures), the remaining unmentioned parts of the lantern — including its **color palette**, **material textures**, **ornamental details**, and **light emission** — must be retained to maintain visual fidelity.
